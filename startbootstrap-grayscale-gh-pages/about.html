About A.S.S.

Based out of Denver, CO, we are an organization dedicated to the obstruction of sunlight in our city. Our founders have been long time proponents of backyard coal burning and open-street tire fires.

Our mission is to bring the smog back to Denver. The sun is a nuisance that needs to be eliminated. We are watching as our precious city is invaded by sun-seekers driving hybrid cars. We want to see our city returned to the people of the darkness: the developers, the vampires, the pale people, basement-dwellers, the people of the night.

This Earth Day, April 22, join us in protest of this ultraviolet scourge at the Denver Environmental Protection Agency.
